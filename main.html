Contrast can be a small element next to a huge one. Contrast can be a highlight color that stands out in a sea of a base color. Contrast could be a thick line vs. thin. It doesn't always have to be dramatic either. Typographic scale involves contrast because it means establishing a hierarchy of type sizes that are noticeably different. Just setting up the sizes for h1-h6 elements is an exercise in contrast.
